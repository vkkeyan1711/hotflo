FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a heat pump and how does it work? +
A heat pump is an energy-efficient device that extracts heat from ambient air and transfers it to water. Unlike an electric geyser that converts electricity directly to heat (1:1 ratio), a heat pump produces 4–5 units of heat for every 1 unit of electricity — making it 4x to 5x more efficient.
How much electricity can I actually save? +
Most hotels save between 65% to 75% on their hot water electricity costs. For a 30-room hotel spending ₹20,000/month on hot water electricity, you could save ₹13,000–₹15,000 every month. Results vary based on usage patterns, water temperature requirement, and local climate.
How many rooms can one heat pump support? +
Hotflo offers systems ranging from 500 LPD (for 10–15 rooms) to 10,000+ LPD (for 200+ rooms). We custom-design the system capacity based on your property's peak hot water demand, number of rooms, and usage patterns. Multiple units can be combined for very large properties.
What is the warranty on Hotflo heat pumps? +
Hotflo heat pumps come with a comprehensive 2-year product warranty and 5-year compressor warranty. We also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) to ensure your system runs at peak efficiency year-round with minimal intervention.
How long does installation take? +
Standard installations for hotels up to 50 rooms are typically completed within 1–2 days. Larger installations may take 3–5 days. Our team handles all plumbing, electrical connections, and commissioning. There is minimal disruption to your hotel operations during installation.
Does a heat pump work during monsoons or cold weather? +
Yes. Hotflo heat pumps are designed to operate efficiently even in temperatures as low as 5°C. In Tamil Nadu's climate, they work at peak efficiency throughout the year, including monsoon season. The systems are weather-sealed and designed for outdoor installation.
